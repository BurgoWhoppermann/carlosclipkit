# Privacy Policy

**Carlo's Clipkit**

*Last updated: February 22, 2026*

## Overview

Carlo's Clipkit is a macOS application for extracting stills, GIFs, and video clips from video files. The app is designed with privacy in mind — all processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

Carlo's Clipkit does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

- Does not collect analytics or usage data
- Does not use tracking or advertising frameworks
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not connect to the internet
- Does not send any data to external servers

## Device Access

The app accesses only:

- **Files you select** — Video files you choose to open and the output folder you choose for exports. No other files are accessed.
- **On-device processing** — Video analysis and scene detection are performed locally using Apple frameworks. No data leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Carlo's Clipkit uses no third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. It relies solely on built-in Apple frameworks (AVFoundation, CoreImage, Vision).

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
